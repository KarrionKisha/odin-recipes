Easy Roast Chicken Breast Recipe

A basic recipe for cooking up a bunch of skinless, boneless chicken breasts.

This is a relatively quick and easy recipe. Roast chicken is an entree that is easy to pair with basically any side dish.

Feel free to spice this recipe up with your favorite spices! (In place of the Creole seasoning)

Ingredients

[4 Servings]

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of coarse sea salt
  • 1 pinch Creole seasoning (such as Tony Chachere'sÂ®), or to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat convection oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Rub chicken breasts with olive oil and sprinkle both sides with salt and Creole seasoning. Place chicken in a broiler pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes. Flip chicken and cook until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 15 minutes more. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  4. Allow chicken to "rest" at least a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
